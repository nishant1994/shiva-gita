CHAPTER 16

THE ELIGIBILITY FOR LIBERATION


[1] Sri Rama Said: O Parameshvara! You have explained in very detail about the path to liberation. Hence, now kindly explain which kind of people become eligible for attaining such liberation. I have great doubts on this matter.

[2] Sri Bhagavan Said: Brahmin, Kshatriya, Vaishya and Shudra. Everyone is eligible for attaining liberation.

[3] Either one is a celibate or a householder or has become a forest dweller, or without going to forest also follows Viraja Diksha, or one who worships me regularly and is my devotee, every such kind of person is eligible for attaining liberation.

[4] However, an atheist, a person of uncleansed heart, who doesn't perform any virtuous deeds, ignorant, and a fake devotee. Such people would not attain liberation.

[5] I am the greatest guru, one who hates me or one who hates Vishnu, such a person even after billions of births would remain in the ocean of sorrow and would not attain liberation.

[6] One who performs rituals or chants my name with the expectation of fruition, that person would gain the desired fruit but cannot attain the final liberation.

[7] One whose senses remain conquered helps one to gain knowledge and do austerities, such a person only gains the fruit of pilgrimage. Others who are not of cleansed heart gain only sins even by staying in those places of pilgrimage.

[8] Every class of people from all castes and creeds have rights on chanting and meditation.

[9] Connecting oneself with me through meditation, the human gets liberated from the world. Therefore, donations, penance, and Vedas are thousand times inferior to meditation.

[10] One who meditates on me while walking, standing, sleeping, or doing any work, even while indulging in sinful activities, that becomes saved from the bondages of the world. There is no beginning or end to meditation. One who meditates on me becomes freed of all fears.

[11] Even a person who is a great sinner, if by any means or due to any reason, just remembers me once or utters the Panchakshari mantra, would gain liberation beyond doubt.

[12] One who sees this entire universe as Shivamaya (of being Shiva's form) need not visit any sacred places of pilgrimage.

[13] Shiva devotees should apply the holy ash and wear Rudraksha on their bodies, this is an important feature for the aspirants of gaining Shiva devotion.

[14] A person who applies holy ash on the body, and wears Rudraksha even if one is a great sinner, would attain liberation. One who doesn't perform any other rituals related to Shiva but only chants Shiva's name continuously, would also attain liberation.

[15] Sage Suta said: In this way, Lord Shiva preached Rama on all the possible subjects and then disappeared.

[16] Disciples said: O great Sage Suta! You have erased our ignorance and blessed us with eternal knowledge. Hence, O Sage! For us you are the only guru. Salutations!
