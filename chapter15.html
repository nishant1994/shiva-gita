CHAPTER 15

THE YOGA OF DEVOTION


[1] Sri Rama said: O Shiva! What is meant by devotion to you? How does it take birth? And how does one gain the supreme happiness called liberation?

[2] Sri Bhagavan said: One who studies scriptures regularly and offers all actions to me, that is my favourite devotee.

[3] One who applies the holy ash all over the body and worships me with devotion; there is no other devotee dearer than this to me.

[4] One who wears Rudraksha beads, one who constantly chants my Panchakshari mantra, both these types of devotees are my favourite and very dear to me.

[5] One who applies holy ash all over the body, sleeps in the ashes conquering wild senses, and recites Sri Rudra hymn with a cleansed heart, and one who recites Atharvasiras hymns, and studies Kaivalya upanishad, such a one attains liberation in the very same life.

[6] I'll tell you the details of the thing which is beyond the dharma and adharma, which is beyond the cause and effect, and which is beyond the past and future. Listen carefully!

[7] The kind of supreme state which is the message of all the Vedas. That is the Omkara, after knowing which people attain the self, which is imperishable.

[8] When compared to a cow, this Omkara is like a bull. This is the one which liberates one from the miserable world.

[9] The Pranava has four syllables; Akara (A), Ukara (U), Makara (M), Ardhamatra (half syllable).

[10] This entire creation with the past, present and future is established in the Omkara.

[11] That which is born, which is yet to be born, everything is Rudra alone. All the life forces reside in the Lord.

[12] Scriptures voice the fact that everything is nondual from Rudra who is the Omkara. That's why Omkara and Shiva are not different. Because the eternal self is established in this Omkara, those who chant Omkara would get liberated for sure beyond any doubts.

[13] With the holy ash obtained from the sacrificial ground, one who worships me by uttering Pranava, there doesn't remain anyone second to such a devotee.

[14] One who worships me with Bilva leaves while uttering Pranava, that becomes my favourite devotee and gets merits multiplied by a factor of billion.

[15] One who is serene, honest, truthful, loves all creatures, remains clean, is a conqueror of his senses, and studies the scriptures to attain knowledge; such a devotee becomes my most loved devotee.

[16] In the early morning, one who goes to Shiva temple and worships me would gain a lot of wealth, prosperity and after death would merge with me.

[17] Either on monday, or on the eleventh day in the fortnight after the full moon day, one who worships me at night by observing fasting, such a devotee would never run into any problems in life.

[18] Whosoever offers me the mixture of milk, curd, sugar, honey and ghee called Panchamrita, water and forest flowers, that becomes very dear to me.

[19] One who stands inside water facing east and raises two hands upwards considering me in the rising sun; one who chants the Panchakshari mantra would enter my abode as easily as one enters own house without any restrictions.

[20] Therefore, O Rama! This knowledge of devotion as preached by me when read by anyone, has the capability to fulfil all desires and gives liberation. If you have further questions to ask, you may do so without hesitation.
